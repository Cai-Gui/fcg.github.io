Welcome to the official website of Fu Cai Gui – Infinite. I am a student at the School of Science and Technology, Singapore, driven by a passion to solve problems and create value. As an entrepreneur, creator, and worldbuilder, I explore technology, design, and the limitless potential of human imagination. This site serves as a central hub for my projects, insights, and social connections, linking visitors to platforms where ideas become reality.

My mission is simple: solve problems, create value, and share what I learn along the way. I write and speak about productivity, focus, and fun, as well as personal growth, futurism, biohacking, and investing. Beyond academics, I am an adventure seeker and a polymath at heart, constantly experimenting across science, art, and digital storytelling. From reverse-origami to forward-looking research, I embrace curiosity as a lifestyle and a strategy for innovation.

I have earned recognition in a variety of national and international arenas, winning the Singapore Science and Engineering Fair, Model United Nations, Samsung Solve for Tomorrow, and the National STEM Championship, among other competitions. My portfolio of work includes the award-winning Hydropod hydrogen generator project, the Artificial Intelligence learning assisstant Scholaris, as well as active involvement in chess and music, where I continue to explore the interplay of creativity, logic, and performance.

In the next twenty years, I believe we will create an intelligence revolution that transforms every aspect of society. With the convergence of artificial intelligence, biotechnology, and quantum computing, humanity will unlock unprecedented levels of progress and opportunity. If you are excited by these possibilities, follow along to explore infinite opportunities together and discover work that blends innovation with a passion for global community building.
